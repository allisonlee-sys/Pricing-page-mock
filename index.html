FAQs

Yes. Amplitude offers two free options. The Starter plan includes 2M events per month, unlimited seats, and every Amplitude product including analytics, session replay, feature flags, experiments, guides and surveys, and AI agents. No credit card is required for Starter. The Free Plus plan also includes 2M events per month but requires a credit card, and you only pay if you exceed the free tier.
Amplitude prices on one metric: events. An event is any user action you choose to track, like a button click, page view, or purchase. All Amplitude products are included in every plan. You choose how many events you need, and session replay, experimentation, and other add-ons are priced as a percentage of your event spend. One number to track, one number to plan around.
Amplitude does not shut off access or lock your data if you exceed your plan. If you go over your included events, you pay the same per-event rate as your plan. There are no penalty rates and no surprise charges. We notify you before any charges apply.
No. The Starter plan is completely free and does not require a credit card. You get 2M events, unlimited seats, and access to every Amplitude product. The Plus plan offers 2M free events but does require a credit card on file, and you are only charged if you exceed the free event threshold.
Yes. Amplitude's full platform is available via MCP (Model Context Protocol). Your coding agents in Claude Code, Cursor, Codex, and other MCP-compatible tools can query the same data your dashboards show. When an agent opens a PR, it can read funnel impact. When it rolls out a flag, it can watch guardrail metrics. Amplitude also offers a CLI Wizard for installing the SDK directly from your terminal.
Yes. The Amplitude CLI Wizard lets you install the SDK, configure your project, and start sending events directly from your terminal. No browser-based setup flow required. Your coding agent can also handle setup through Amplitude's MCP integration and instrumentation skills.
Amplitude's Startup Program gives qualifying startups the full Enterprise plan free for 12 months, a value of more than $50,000. You qualify if your company has fewer than 20 employees and has raised less than $10M. The program includes every Amplitude product, premium support, and enterprise-grade features at no cost for the first year.
Amplitude and PostHog both offer analytics, session replay, feature flags, and experiments. Amplitude differentiates in three areas. First, Amplitude connects error data, session replays, and behavioral analytics to individual users, not just request counts, so you can measure user impact rather than event volume. Second, Amplitude's experimentation includes sequential testing, CUPED variance reduction, and auto-rollback on guardrail breaches as standard features. Third, Amplitude prices on one metric (events) with everything included, while PostHog prices each product separately with individual free tiers and usage curves.
